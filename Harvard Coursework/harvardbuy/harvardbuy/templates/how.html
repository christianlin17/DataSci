The easiest way to buy and sell stuff at Harvard.

1. The problem

Selling your stuff on campus is difficult. House mailing lists. Free and for sale. Asking your friends if they know anyone that might be interested in buying your gray futon...not very efficient. We think that selling your items at Harvard, whether during or at the end of the year (hey seniors) should not be a hassle.


2. The solution

HarvardBuy makes buying and selling your items easy. Create an account in seconds, and you will be able to sell your stuff to the entire Harvard College community (that's more than 6,000 potential buyers!). Unlike house mailing lists, HarvardBuy lets buyers navigate items by category and easily find the perfect item. Items listed on HarvardBuy expire after 5 days if unsold: everything you see on the website is still for sale. No "sorry already sold :(" anymore.
